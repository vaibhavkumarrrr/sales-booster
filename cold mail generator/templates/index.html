Cold Email Generator

Welcome to the Cold Email Generator! This tool helps you craft personalized and professional cold emails for job applications. Simply enter the job listing URL below, and the tool will analyze the job description, extract key skills and responsibilities, and generate a compelling email tailored to the job. It also includes relevant portfolio links to showcase your expertise.

How to Use:
1. Copy the job listing URL from the job board.
2. Paste the URL into the input field below.
3. Click "Generate Email" and wait for the magic to happen!
4. Review the generated email and customize it further if needed.
